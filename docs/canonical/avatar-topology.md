# Avatar Topology

This document freezes the scaffold shape for avatar identity so the Strategist, Council, support specialists, and interns remain separate operational roles.

## Roles

- `strategist`: session orchestrator with gated reflection after synthesis and high-priority Foundation access.
- `council`: debate participants that produce positions and synthesis support under session-scoped reflection gates.
- `support_specialist`: non-Council specialists such as QA, measurement, or channel operators that attach to artifacts, alerts, or campaign feedback.
- `intern`: delegated research executors with supervised return paths and reduced authority.

## Registry expectations

- Every avatar must have a stable `avatar_key`.
- Every avatar must declare a role, office zone, reflection profile, and optional support domain.
- Every avatar is backed by PRL `agent_essences` state and EEL lattice state.
- Frontend roster projections may cache registry data, but the canonical source belongs to backend contracts and storage.

## Reflection gates

- Strategist reflection is gated to post-synthesis or explicit review windows.
- Council reflection is gated to session-close or operator replay.
- Support-specialist reflection is gated to artifact feedback or performance loops.
- Intern reflection is supervisor-reviewed and never autonomous.
